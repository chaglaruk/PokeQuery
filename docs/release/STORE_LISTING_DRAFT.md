## Short Description
Build safe, powerful search strings for Pokémon GO cleanup instantly.

## Full Description
PokeQuery is your offline-first companion tool for building safe, custom search strings to manage your Pokémon GO storage. Whether you need to quickly find duplicates for transfer, locate safe trade candidates, or hunt down perfect 4★ catches, PokeQuery generates the exact search string you need. Just tap to copy, and paste it directly into your game!

**Features:**
- **Safe Cleanup Generator**: Target low-value 1★ candidates without risking your shinies or legendaries.
- **2x Candy Prep**: Find exact duplicates by species while protecting your favorites.
- **Trade Fodder Finder**: Locate non-shiny, non-traded candidates quickly.
- **Untagged Organizer**: Find everything missing a tag.
- **Expert Builder**: Write raw strings with built-in safety linting to prevent accidental transfers.
- **Favorites Library**: Save your most-used strings locally for instant access.
