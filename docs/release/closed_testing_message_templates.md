# Closed Testing Message Templates

Ready-to-use templates for recruiting and managing PokeQuery closed testers. **Use placeholders, never private real emails.** Replace `[OPT-IN URL]` with the closed-testing opt-in link and `[GOOGLE GROUP LINK]` with the tester Google Group invite.

---

## 1. Reddit tester recruitment post

**Title:** Closed testers wanted: PokeQuery — a free, offline Pokémon GO search-string helper (no login, no ads)

**Body:**

>
> It's currently in **Google Play closed testing** and I need a few testers to help before I can request production access.
>
> **What you'd do:**
> - Join the tester Google Group: [GOOGLE GROUP LINK]
> - Opt in: [OPT-IN URL]
> - Install from Play (closed track), open the app, try 2–3 goals, and copy/paste a string into Pokémon GO
> - Send brief feedback via Settings → "Send tester feedback" (it just opens your email app) or DM me
>
> The app is independent and not affiliated with Niantic/The Pokémon Company/Nintendo. Turkish-language output is labeled beta and still being verified. Estimated effort: ~10–15 minutes.
>
> Thanks!
