## Columns

- **Reddit username** — public handle used for recruitment (safe to record).
- **Email / Google account** — keep private; record only a checkmark or `(private)` here.
- **Country** — useful for locale/language feedback.
- **Joined Google Group?** — required to be eligible for the closed track.
- **Opted in?** — accepted the closed-testing opt-in URL.
- **Installed?** — installed the closed-track build from Play.
- **Opened app?** — at least one launch recorded (cross-check with their feedback).
- **Feedback received?** — any structured feedback (in-app mailto or DM).
- **I tested back?** — did you acknowledge/respond to their feedback?
- **Still opted in Day 7? / Day 14?** — retention for the continuous-tester requirement.
- **Notes** — which goals they tested, issues, locale.

## Reminder

Google's production-access requirements (2024+) for personal developer accounts generally ask for a meaningful closed test with retained testers over ~14 days. Keep this table current and honest.
