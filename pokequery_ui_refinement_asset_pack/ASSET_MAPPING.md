## Docs-only reference
- `reference_ui_target.png`
  - Visual north star. Match it closely in structure and polish, but do **not** add Pokémon or creature art.

## Important design rule
Do **not** create or keep fictional monsters, Pokémon-like creatures, or animal characters. The app should look like the reference through layout, iconography, typography, glow treatment, chips, and component polish — not through creature art.
